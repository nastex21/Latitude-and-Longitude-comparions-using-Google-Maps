Metro Cities with Similar Latitudes or Longitudes

By entering latitude and longitude values or by clicking on the "Set My Location" button, you can now see markers on the map of similar latitude or longitude coordinates of big metropolitan areas (with a population of greater than one million people) from around the world. You can also adjust the latitude and longitude values using the adjuster by subtracting or adding up to 10 degrees. Data will also appear on the right of your screen.


Source of population data: http://www.demographia.com/db-worldua.pdf
